Baked Macaroni and Cheese

A creamy and savory dish for cheese lovers everwhere.

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages sharp cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
  • 1 (8 ounce) package mild cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 3/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and spray 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray
  2. Cook elbow macaroni according to directions
  3. Set aside 1/2 cup mild shredded cheese
  4. Mix remaining cheeses, sour cream, cottage cheese, and egg in a bowl. Then stir in cooked macaroni and pour into baking dish. Cover with cheese that was set aside.
  5. Bake for about 40 minutes and then let cool for 10 minutes
