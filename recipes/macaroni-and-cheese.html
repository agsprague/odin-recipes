Baked Macaroni and Cheese

A creamy and savory dish for cheese lovers everwhere.

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages sharp cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 3/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • salt and ground black pepper
