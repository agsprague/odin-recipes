Chicken Alfredo

Slow Cooker Chicken Alfredo

An hearty and delicious main dish that fits into a busy schedule.

Ingredients:

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
  • 2 (16 ounce) jars alfredo sauce
  • 4 slices swiss cheese
  • 3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix chicken cubes and alfredo sauce in crock pot and cook for 2 hours
  2. Add parmesan and swiss cheese and cook for another 30 minutes
  3. Season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper
